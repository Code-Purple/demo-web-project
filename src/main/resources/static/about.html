About Karaokin':

Welcome to Karaokin' - a web service for all of your karaoke needs.

We are Code-Purple and we are dedicated to providing our users with the resources and tools for the ultimate karaoke experience. Our service delivers the textual lyrics and corresponding audio tracks to any songs found within our growing library of artists and their work.

Portable

There is no need to be restricted to a desktop or laptop to take advantage of our service. Our site is designed to be compatible with most mobile devices as well. Whether you have a smart phone or a tablet, we ensure that you have easy access to Karaokin' on-the-go.

Easy

Searching for a song is a breeze. Simply start typing the name of your desired song in the search box provided and a list of the closest matches will appear below your query for your convenience. Karaokin' grants you the entire karaoke experience through a simple, yet elegant design all at the touch of your fingertips.
